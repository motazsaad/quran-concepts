Chapter Name:Ar-Rahman Verse No:35

 يُرْسَلُ عَلَيْكُمَا شُوَاظٌ مِّن نَّارٍ وَنُحَاسٌ فَلَا تَنتَصِرَانِ {35
055:035 Khan
:
 There will be sent against you both, smokeless flames of fire and (molten) brass, and you will not be able to defend yourselves.
055:035 Maulana
:
 The flames of fire and sparks of brass will be sent upon you, then you will not be able to defend yourselves.
055:035 Pickthal
:
 There will be sent, against you both, heat of fire and flash of brass, and ye will not escape.
055:035 Rashad
:
 You get bombarded with projectiles of fire and metal, and you cannot win.
055:035 Sarwar
:
 Flames of fire and molten brass will be released against you and you will not be able to protect yourselves.
055:035 Shakir
:
 The flames of fire and smoke will be sent on you two, then you will not be able to defend yourselves.
055:035 Sherali
:
 There shall be sent against you a flame of fire, and molten copper; and you shall not be able to help yourselves.
055:035 Yusufali
:
 On you will be sent (O ye evil ones twain!) a flame of fire (to burn) and a smoke (to choke): no defence will ye have:
