Chapter Name:Az-Zumar Verse No:62

 اللَّهُ خَالِقُ كُلِّ شَيْءٍ وَهُوَ عَلَى كُلِّ شَيْءٍ وَكِيلٌ {62
039:062 Khan
:
 Allah is the Creator of all things, and He is the Wakil (Trustee, Disposer of affairs, Guardian, etc.) over all things.
039:062 Maulana
:
 Allah is the Creator of all things and He has charge over everything.
039:062 Pickthal
:
 Allah is Creator of all things, and He is Guardian over all things.
039:062 Rashad
:
 GOD is the Creator of all things, and He is in full control of all things.
039:062 Sarwar
:
 God is the Creator and Guardian of all things.
039:062 Shakir
:
 Allah is the Creator of every thing and He has charge over every thing.
039:062 Sherali
:
 ALLAH is the creator of all things, and HE is Guardian over all things.
039:062 Yusufali
:
 Allah is the Creator of all things, and He is the Guardian and Disposer of all affairs.
