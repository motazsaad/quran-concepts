Chapter Name:Al-Qamar Verse No:40

 وَلَقَدْ يَسَّرْنَا الْقُرْآنَ لِلذِّكْرِ فَهَلْ مِن مُّدَّكِرٍ {40
054:040 Khan
:
 And indeed, We have made the Qur'an easy to understand and remember, then is there any that will remember (or receive admonition)?
054:040 Maulana
:
 And certainly We have made the Qur'an easy to remember, but is there any one who will mind?
054:040 Pickthal
:
 And in truth We have made the Qur'an easy to remember; but is there any that remembereth?
054:040 Rashad
:
 We made the Quran easy to learn. Does any of you wish to learn?
054:040 Sarwar
:
 We have made the Quran easy to understand, but is there anyone who would pay attention?.
054:040 Shakir
:
 And certainly We have made the Quran easy for remembrance, but is there anyone who will mind?
054:040 Sherali
:
 And, indeed, WE have made the Qur'an easy to remember. But is there anyone who will take heed?
054:040 Yusufali
:
 And We have indeed made the Qur'an easy to understand and remember: then is there any that will receive admonition?
