Chapter Name:Ar-Rahman Verse No:9

 وَأَقِيمُوا الْوَزْنَ بِالْقِسْطِ وَلَا تُخْسِرُوا الْمِيزَانَ {9
055:009 Khan
:
 And observe the weight with equity and do not make the balance deficient.
055:009 Maulana
:
 And keep up the balance with equity, nor fall short in the measure.
055:009 Pickthal
:
 But observe the measure strictly, nor fall short thereof.
055:009 Rashad
:
 You shall establish justice; do not violate the law.
055:009 Sarwar
:
 Therefore, maintain just measure and do not transgress against the Balance.
055:009 Shakir
:
 And keep up the balance with equity and do not make the measure deficient.
055:009 Sherali
:
 So weigh all things with justice and fall not short of the measure.
055:009 Yusufali
:
 So establish weight with justice and fall not short in the balance.
