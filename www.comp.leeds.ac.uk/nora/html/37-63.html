Chapter Name:As-Saaffat Verse No:63

 إِنَّا جَعَلْنَاهَا فِتْنَةً لِّلظَّالِمِينَ {63
037:063 Khan
:
 Truly We have made it (as) a trail for the Zalimun (polytheists, disbelievers, wrong-doers, etc.).
037:063 Maulana
:
 Surely We have made it a trial for the wrongdoers.
037:063 Pickthal
:
 Lo! We have appointed it a torment for wrong-doers.
037:063 Rashad
:
 We have rendered it a punishment for the transgressors.
037:063 Sarwar
:
 which We have made as a torment for the unjust?.
037:063 Shakir
:
 Surely We have made it to be a trial to the unjust.
037:063 Sherali
:
 Verily, WE have made it a trial for the wrongdoers.
037:063 Yusufali
:
 For We have truly made it (as) a trial for the wrong-doers.
