002:156 Khan

 Who, when afflicted with calamity, say: "Truly! To Allah we belong and truly, to Him we shall return."
002:156 Maulana

002:156 Sarwar

 Who, when a misfortune befalls them, say: Surely we are Allah's and to Him we shall surely return.
