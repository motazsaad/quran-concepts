Chapter Name:Ad-Dukhan Verse No:31

 مِن فِرْعَوْنَ إِنَّهُ كَانَ عَالِيًا مِّنَ الْمُسْرِفِينَ {31
044:031 Khan
:
 From Fir'aun (Pharaoh); Verily! He was arrogant and was of the Musrifun (those who transgress beyond bound in spending and other things and commit great sins).
044:031 Maulana
:
 From Pharaoh. Surely he was haughty, prodigal.
044:031 Pickthal
:
 (We delivered them) from Pharaoh. Lo! he was a tyrant of the wanton ones.
044:031 Rashad
:
 From Pharaoh; he was a tyrant.
044:031 Sarwar
:
 and from the Pharaoh. He was the chief of the transgressors.
044:031 Shakir
:
 From Firon; surely he was haughty, (and) one of the extravagant.
044:031 Sherali
:
 Inflicted by Pharaoh. He was, surely, haughty even among the extravagant.
044:031 Yusufali
:
 Inflicted by Pharaoh, for he was arrogant (even) among inordinate transgressors.
