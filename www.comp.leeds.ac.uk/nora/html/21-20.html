Chapter Name:Al-Anbiya Verse No:20

 يُسَبِّحُونَ اللَّيْلَ وَالنَّهَارَ لَا يَفْتُرُونَ {20
021:020 Khan
:
 They (i.e. the angels) glorify His Praises night and day, (and) they never slacken (to do so).
021:020 Maulana
:
 They glorify (Him) night and day: they flag not.
021:020 Pickthal
:
 They glorify (Him) night and day; they flag not.
021:020 Rashad
:
 They glorify night and day, without ever tiring.
021:020 Sarwar
:
 They glorify Him day and night without fail.
021:020 Shakir
:
 They glorify (Him) by night and day; they are never languid.
021:020 Sherali
:
 They glorify HIM night and day; and they flag not.
021:020 Yusufali
:
 They celebrate His praises night and day, nor do they ever flag or intermit.
