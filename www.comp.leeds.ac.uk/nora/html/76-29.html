Chapter Name:Al-Insan Verse No:29

 إِنَّ هَذِهِ تَذْكِرَةٌ فَمَن شَاء اتَّخَذَ إِلَى رَبِّهِ سَبِيلًا {29
076:029 Khan
:
 Verily! This (Verses of the Qur'an) is an admonition, so whosoever wills, let him take a Path to his Lord (Allah).
076:029 Maulana
:
 Surely this is a Reminder; so whoever will, let him take a way to his Lord.
076:029 Pickthal
:
 Lo! this is an Admonishment, that whosoever will may choose a way unto his Lord.
076:029 Rashad
:
 This is a reminder: whoever wills shall choose the path to his Lord.
076:029 Sarwar
:
 This chapter is a reminder. Let those who want, seek guidance from their Lord.
076:029 Shakir
:
 Surely this is a reminder, so whoever pleases takes to his Lord a way.
076:029 Sherali
:
 Verily, this is a Reminder. So whoever wishes, may take a way unto his Lord.
076:029 Yusufali
:
 This is an admonition: Whosoever will, let him take a (straight) Path to his Lord.
