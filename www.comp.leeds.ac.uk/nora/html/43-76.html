Chapter Name:Az-Zukhruf Verse No:76

 وَمَا ظَلَمْنَاهُمْ وَلَكِن كَانُوا هُمُ الظَّالِمِينَ {76
043:076 Khan
:
 We wronged them not, but they were the Zalimun (polytheists, wrong-doers, etc.).
043:076 Maulana
:
 And We wronged them not but they were themselves the wrongdoers.
043:076 Pickthal
:
 We wronged them not, but they it was who did the wrong.
043:076 Rashad
:
 It is not us who wronged them; it is they who wronged their own souls.
043:076 Sarwar
:
 They will cry out, "Guard, let your Lord destroy us".
043:076 Shakir
:
 And We are not unjust to them, but they themselves were unjust.
043:076 Sherali
:
 And WE wronged them not, but it was they themselves who were the wrongdoers.
043:076 Yusufali
:
 Nowise shall We be unjust to them: but it is they who have been unjust themselves.
