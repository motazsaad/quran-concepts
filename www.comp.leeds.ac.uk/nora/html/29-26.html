 So Lout (Lot) believed in him [Ibrahim's (Abraham) Message of Islamic Monotheism]. He [Ibrahim (Abraham)] said: "I will emigrate for the sake of my Lord. Verily, He is the All- Mighty, the All-Wise."
029:026 Maulana

029:026 Pickthal

029:026 Rashad
