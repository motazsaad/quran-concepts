023:086 Khan
:
 Say: "Who is (the) Lord of the seven heavens, and (the) Lord of the Great Throne?"
023:086 Maulana

 Say: Who is the Lord of the seven heavens and the Lord of the mighty Throne of power?
023:086 Pickthal
:
 Say: Who is Lord of the seven heavens, and Lord of the Tremendous Throne?
023:086 Rashad
:
 Say, "Who is the Lord of the seven universes; the Lord of the great dominion?"
023:086 Sarwar
:
 Ask them, "Who is the Lord of the seven heavens and the Great Throne?".
023:086 Shakir
:
 Say: Who is the Lord of the seven heavens and the Lord of the mighty dominion?
023:086 Sherali
:
 Say, 'Who is the Lord of the seven Heavens, and the Lord of the Great Throne?'
023:086 Yusufali
:
 Say: "Who is the Lord of the seven heavens, and the Lord of the Throne (of Glory) Supreme?"
