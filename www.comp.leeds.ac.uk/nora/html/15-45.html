Chapter Name:Al-Hijr Verse No:45

 إِنَّ الْمُتَّقِينَ فِي جَنَّاتٍ وَعُيُونٍ {45
015:045 Khan
:
 "Truly! The Muttaqun (pious and righteous persons - see V.2:2) will be amidst Gardens and water-springs (Paradise).
015:045 Maulana
:
 Surely those who keep their duty are in Gardens and fountains.
015:045 Pickthal
:
 Lo! those who ward off (evil) are among gardens and watersprings.
015:045 Rashad
:
 As for the righteous, they will enjoy gardens and springs.
015:045 Sarwar
:
 The pious will live in gardens with streams
015:045 Shakir
:
 Surely those who guard (against evil) shall be in the midst of gardens and fountains:
015:045 Sherali
:
 Verily, the righteous, will be placed amid gardens and fountains.
015:045 Yusufali
:
 The righteous (will be) amid gardens and fountains (of clear-flowing water).
