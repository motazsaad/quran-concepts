Chapter Name:Al-Maeda Verse No:98

 اعْلَمُواْ أَنَّ اللّهَ شَدِيدُ الْعِقَابِ وَأَنَّ اللّهَ غَفُورٌ رَّحِيمٌ {98
005:098 Khan
:
 Know that Allah is Severe in punishment and that Allah is Oft-Forgiving, Most Merciful.
005:098 Maulana
:
 Know that Allah is severe in requiting (evil) and that Allah is Forgiving, Merciful.
005:098 Pickthal
:
 Know that Allah is severe in punishment, but that Allah (also) is Forgiving, Merciful.
005:098 Rashad
:
 Know that GOD is strict in enforcing retribution, and that GOD is Forgiving, Most Merciful.
005:098 Sarwar
:
 Know that God is stern in His retribution and He is All-forgiving and All-merciful.
005:098 Shakir
:
 Know that Allah is severe in requiting (evil) and that Allah is Forgiving, Merciful.
005:098 Sherali
:
 Know that ALLAH is Severe in punishment and that ALLAH is also Most Forgiving and ever Merciful.
005:098 Yusufali
:
 Know ye that Allah is strict in punishment and that Allah is Oft-forgiving, Most Merciful.
