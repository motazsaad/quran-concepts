Chapter Name:As-Saaffat Verse No:121

 إِنَّا كَذَلِكَ نَجْزِي الْمُحْسِنِينَ {121
037:121 Khan
:
 Verily, thus do We reward the Muhsinun (good- doers - see V.2:112).
037:121 Maulana
:
 Thus do We reward the doers of good.
037:121 Pickthal
:
 Lo! thus do We reward the good.
037:121 Rashad
:
 We thus reward the righteous.
037:121 Sarwar
:
 Thus do We reward the righteous ones.
037:121 Shakir
:
 Even thus do We reward the doers of good.
037:121 Sherali
:
 Thus, indeed, do WE reward those who do good.
037:121 Yusufali
:
 Thus indeed do We reward those who do right.
