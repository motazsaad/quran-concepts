Chapter Name:Al-Waqia Verse No:75

 فَلَا أُقْسِمُ بِمَوَاقِعِ النُّجُومِ {75
056:075 Khan
:
 So I swear by Mawaqi (setting or the mansions, etc.) of the stars (they traverse).
056:075 Maulana
:
 But nay, I swear by revelation of portions (of the Qur'an)! --
056:075 Pickthal
:
 Nay, I swear by the places of the stars -
056:075 Rashad
:
 I swear by the positions of the stars.
056:075 Sarwar
:
 I do not need to swear by the setting of the stars
056:075 Shakir
:
 But nay! I swear by the falling of stars;
056:075 Sherali
:
 Nay, I cite as proof the shooting of the stars -
056:075 Yusufali
:
 Furthermore I call to witness the setting of the Stars,-
