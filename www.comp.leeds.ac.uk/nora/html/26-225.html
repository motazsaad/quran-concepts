Chapter Name:Al-Shuara Verse No:225

 أَلَمْ تَرَ أَنَّهُمْ فِي كُلِّ وَادٍ يَهِيمُونَ {225
026:225 Khan
:
 See you not that they speak about every subject (praising others right or wrong) in their poetry?
026:225 Maulana
:
 Seest thou not that they wander in every valley,
026:225 Pickthal
:
 Hast thou not seen how they stray in every valley,
026:225 Rashad
:
 Do you not see that their loyalty shifts according to the situation?
026:225 Sarwar
:
 Have you not seen them wandering and bewildered in every valley
026:225 Shakir
:
 Do you not see that they wander about bewildered in every valley?
026:225 Sherali
:
 Dost thou not see how they wonder aimlessly in every valley,
026:225 Yusufali
:
 Seest thou not that they wander distracted in every valley?-
