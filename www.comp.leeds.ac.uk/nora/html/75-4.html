Chapter Name:Al-Qiyama Verse No:4

 بَلَى قَادِرِينَ عَلَى أَن نُّسَوِّيَ بَنَانَهُ {4
075:004 Khan
:
 Yes, We are Able to put together in perfect order the tips of his fingers.
075:004 Maulana
:
 Yea, We are Powerful to make complete his whole make.
075:004 Pickthal
:
 Yea, verily. We are Able to restore his very fingers!
075:004 Rashad
:
 Yes indeed; we are able to reconstruct his finger tip.
075:004 Sarwar
:
 We certainly have the power to restore them even the very tips of their finger.
075:004 Shakir
:
 Yea! We are able to make complete his very fingertips
075:004 Sherali
:
 Yea, WE have the power to restore his very finger-tips.
075:004 Yusufali
:
 Nay, We are able to put together in perfect order the very tips of his fingers.
