 أَلَيْسَ ذَلِكَ بِقَادِرٍ عَلَى أَن يُحْيِيَ الْمَوْتَى {40
075:040 Khan
:
 Is not He (Allah Who does that), Able to give life to the dead? (Yes! He is Able to do all things).
075:040 Maulana
:
 Is not He powerful to give life to the dead?
075:040 Pickthal
:
 Is not He (Who doeth so) Able to bring the dead to life?
075:040 Rashad
:
 Is He then unable to revive the dead?
075:040 Sarwar
:
 Does He then not have the power to bring the dead back to life?.
075:040 Shakir
:
 Is not He able to give life to the dead?
075:040 Sherali
:
 Has not such a one the power to raise the dead to life?
075:040 Yusufali
:
 Has not He, (the same), the power to give life to the dead?
