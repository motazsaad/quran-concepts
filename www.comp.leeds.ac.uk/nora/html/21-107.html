Chapter Name:Al-Anbiya Verse No:107

 وَمَا أَرْسَلْنَاكَ إِلَّا رَحْمَةً لِّلْعَالَمِينَ {107
021:107 Khan
:
 And We have sent you (O Muhammad SAW) not but as a mercy for the 'Alamin (mankind, jinns and all that exists).
021:107 Maulana
:
 And We have not sent thee but as a mercy to the nations.
021:107 Pickthal
:
 We sent thee not save as a mercy for the peoples.
021:107 Rashad
:
 We have sent you out of mercy from us towards the whole world.
021:107 Sarwar
:
 (Muhammad), We have sent you for no other reason but to be a mercy for mankind.
021:107 Shakir
:
 And We have not sent you but as a mercy to the worlds.
021:107 Sherali
:
 And WE have not sent thee but as a mercy for all peoples.
021:107 Yusufali
:
 We sent thee not, but as a Mercy for all creatures.
